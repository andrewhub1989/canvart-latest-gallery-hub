## What CanvArt Is

CanvArt gives people a straightforward way to spend time with visual art online while staying connected to an active creative community. It combines viewing, publishing, and discovery inside a single web experience, so users can move naturally from browsing artwork to sharing their own.

The platform suits artists, photographers, and other creators who want their work presented alongside community submissions. With its gallery-first layout and discovery built around participation, it supports both casual visitors and people who return regularly to contribute.
